RESNET18 TRANSFER LEARNING FOR AUDIO CLASSIFICATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DATASET:
  - ESC-50: 8400 samples, 50 classes
  - Train: 7680 samples (augmented)
  - Val: 320 samples
  - Test: 400 samples
  - SpecAugment: ENABLED for training

MODEL:
  - Architecture: ResNet18 (pretrained on ImageNet)
  - Total parameters: 11,202,162
  - Trainable parameters: 11,202,162
  - Input: Mel Spectrogram (128x128) converted to 3-channel RGB
  - Dropout: 0.5

TRAINING STRATEGY:
  - Stage 1: FC layer only (20 epochs)
    Best Val Acc: 39.69%
  
  - Stage 2: Full fine-tuning (33 epochs)
    Best Val Acc: 86.56%
  
  - Total epochs: 53
  - Optimizer: Adam (Stage 1: lr=0.001, Stage 2: lr=0.0001)
  - Scheduler: ReduceLROnPlateau
  - Early stopping: patience=15

RESULTS:
  - Train Accuracy: 98.75%
  - Val Accuracy: 86.56%
  - Test Accuracy: 86.50%
  - Improvement vs SVM: +13.44%
  - Improvement vs CNN: +7.79%
